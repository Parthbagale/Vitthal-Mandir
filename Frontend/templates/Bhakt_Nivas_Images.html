About Bhakt Nivas

Shri Vitthal Rukmini Mandir Samiti has built modern lodging facilities for devotees. IGBC has ranked this building and this is the first "Green Building" in the district. It has capacity for 1200 devotees. Special types of trees having religious and health significance are planted. Water from this building is not sent out but is processed and re-used. Rain water and waste water will be processed and will be used for trees in the building premise.

Garbage is collected at one place in rooms. Dry and wet garbage will be separated and will be processed to produce fertilizers. For this purpose, a modern machine is installed in the building. Modern fire safety mechanisms are used in the building to control fire. In case of fire, within a few seconds oxygen and water will be sprayed. Heavy water sprays will be triggered where fire intensity is high.

Bhakta Nivas has trees like Tulasi, Zendu, Red flower, and 27 trees belonging to 27 Nakshatras. Nakshatra trees were planted on that particular Nakshatra.

Other Facilities:

  • A big veg restaurant with all modern facilities
  • 2200 SqMeter lawn with 15×9 Meter stage for various programs
  • 43 shops on front side of Bhakta Nivas
  • Parking for 273 four wheelers and 200 two wheelers
